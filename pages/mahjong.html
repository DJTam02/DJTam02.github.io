My Mahjong Game

One of my favourite games to play with my friends is Mahjong (without the betting of course). However, I don't have a Mahjong table or even a Mahjong set!

A solution that I came up with was to make my own set! But virtually.

My virutal Mahjong set allow us to play without having to find a date where we are all available.

With the implementation of AI with three levels of difficulty, it also gives us to flexibility to play with 1 to 4 players.
